The Build

How a BSD kitchen gets made

Four stages, start to finish — the same order every time, because skipping one is how kitchens end up crooked.

01

Design & Measure

We come to you for exact measurements, talk through the layout in detail, and map out the materials before a single board is cut.

02

Material Selection

You choose the species and finish from real slabs in our shop — not a swatch card. What you touch is what gets installed.

03

Build

Dovetailed drawer boxes, inset doors, hand-rubbed finishes. Built and dry-fit in our shop before it ever reaches your kitchen.

04

Install & Walkthrough

Precision fit, plumb and level checked twice, and a full walkthrough of your new cabinetry before we call the job done.
